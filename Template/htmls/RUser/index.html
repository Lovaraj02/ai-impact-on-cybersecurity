AI, e-Governance & Cybersecurity Summary

  • AI is vital in protecting systems from cyber threats like malware and phishing.
  • Strengthens cybersecurity for governments and organizations via e-Governance.
  • Relationship between AI, e-Governance, and cybersecurity is context-specific.
  • Stakeholders with different expertise influence this relationship.
  • Study found e-Governance partially mediates the impact of AI on cybersecurity.
  • Stakeholder involvement moderates AI → e-Governance and e-Governance → Cybersecurity links.
  • All stakeholders value secure, transparent cyberspace for e-services.
  • Governments can apply these findings to improve smart city cybersecurity.
